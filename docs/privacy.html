Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Overview

PNG to Slides ("the App") is a Canva app that inserts PNG images into presentations. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not have a backend server, does not use analytics, and does not track usage.

3. How Your Images Are Processed

When you select PNG files:

  • Files are read locally in your browser using the JavaScript FileReader API
  • Images are uploaded to Canva's servers through the official Canva Apps SDK (upload() API) to be inserted into your design
  • No copies of your images are sent to any third-party servers
  • No image data is stored by the App after insertion

4. Third-Party Services

The App runs within the Canva platform and uses Canva's APIs. Your use of Canva is subject to Canva's Privacy Policy. The App itself does not integrate with any other third-party services.

5. Data Storage

The App does not store any data locally or remotely. File selections are held in browser memory only during your active session and are cleared when you close the app or refresh the page.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies, local storage, or any tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App collects no personal data from any user.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
